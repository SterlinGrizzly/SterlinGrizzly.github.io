Artist Statement

I find professionalism to be the forefront of work, but expressionism to be at the top of art, when chosen to work I try to bring both into question; However stuck to one I may be, there's an expectancy of the other accompanying. Whereas strict behavior is a natural born attitude of mine, entertainment is a second nature to my demeanor, when on the canvas I shine my paint boldly, and when on stage I present myself loose.
