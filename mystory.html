About Sterling Grizzly

I am an artist, performer, and actor with a passion for bringing creativity to life. I have been cultivating my experience in multiple fields of art, creating an unique style of illustration and painting, being able to perform theatrical plays to perfection, and performing outstandingly with my violin.

During my free time, I enjoy reading, creating masks, writing, and drawing. I love to create worlds of my own, and I want to put my vision into everything I do. I am ready for the world to come, and it better be ready for me.
